Add New Device

1

Open Lenovo Vantage

Search for "Vantage" in the Windows Start menu and open the Lenovo Vantage application.

2

Navigate to Home

Click on "Home" in the left sidebar. Your device information is displayed on the home screen.

3

Locate Your Device Details

Find your device card (for example, "ThinkPad X9-15 Gen 1"). Look for these two important numbers:

  • Serial number (e.g., PF5AWM6A)
  • Product number (e.g., 21Q7ZCM0US)
4

Copy the Information

Click the "Copy all" button to copy all device details at once, or manually select and copy each number individually.

5

Add to Your Settings

Return to this settings page, click the "Add Device" button above, and enter your Product Number and Serial Number.

Tip: If you don't have Lenovo Vantage installed, you can download it for free from the Microsoft Store.
